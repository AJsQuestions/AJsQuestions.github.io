Science moves forward on the shoulders of broken experiments.

---

### Factor zoo replication (2023)

I tried to rerun a famous factor paper on newer data. The signals looked great until I realized the cleaning code was creating them. I learned to be suspicious of heroic Sharpe ratios that survive every tweak.

---

### Reinforcement learning for trading (2022)

Fun to build, impossible to explain. The policy worked in backtests but turned opaque in live debugging. It taught me why some clever ideas stay lab toys and why interpretability isn't optional when real money is involved.

---

I share these to normalize exploration—and to remind myself that "no" is still progress.
