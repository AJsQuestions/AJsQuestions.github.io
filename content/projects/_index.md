# Projects

Side projects are where theory meets impatience.

---

## Spotify as a Behavioral Dataset

I built a full Python pipeline to treat my Spotify library as panel data:  
- playlist embeddings  
- genre drift over time  
- monthly consumption dynamics

This started as curiosity and turned into a lesson  
on clustering, identity, and overfitting your own tastes.

---

## Financial ML Teaching Notebooks

Reusable Jupyter notebooks explaining:  
- bias–variance tradeoff *visually*  
- why regularization feels like “belief shrinkage”  
- how factor models sneak into ML regressions

Originally built while TA‑ing at Chicago Booth.  
Now evolving into open teaching material.

## Math Notes for Economists

A growing set of dense, diagram‑heavy notes covering:  
- stochastic calculus

Written for people who want intuition *and* rigor,  
and don’t trust textbooks that skip steps.
